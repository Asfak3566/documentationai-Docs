## Overview

To create documentation in Ashfaq afr:

1. Log in at `https://ashfaqafr.com/login`.
2. Click "New Project" on your dashboard.
3. Enter your project name.
4. Choose a template or start from scratch.
5. Click "Create" to begin editing.

## Tip

You can collaborate with team members by inviting them under "Project Settings".
