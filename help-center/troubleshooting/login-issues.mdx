---

## Symptoms
- Unable to access dashboard
- Error: "Invalid credentials" or timeout

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your credentials">
    Make sure username and password are correct. Reset password if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browser troubleshooting">
    Clear browser cache and cookies. Try a private/incognito window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If still stuck, email support@ashfaqafr.com for help.
  </Step>
</Steps>
