## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your project">
    Log in and select your project from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new page">
    Click "Add Page", enter a title, and select the location in your docs tree.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write and format content">
    Use the editor to add text, images, and code snippets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click "Publish" to make your page live for your audience.
  </Step>
</Steps>
