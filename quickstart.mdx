## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account verification
- Access to your email inbox for confirmation links
- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
</Callout>

## Sign Up for an Account

Follow these steps to create your Ashfaq afr account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://app.ashfaqafr.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email address, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Ashfaq afr and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and access your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a New Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up your first space to organize project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    From the main dashboard, click **New Space** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, select visibility (`Private` or `Public`), and add a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="save">
    Click **Create**. You'll be redirected to your new space's overview page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Document

Ashfaq afr supports multiple ways to create documents. Choose your preferred method.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown Editor" icon="edit-3">
    Click **New Document** > **Blank MDX**.

    Use the built-in editor to write content:

````mdx
## Welcome to My Docs

This is your first document in Ashfaq afr.

- List item 1
- List item 2
````

    Preview changes in real-time and publish.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import from GitHub" icon="github">
    Select **New Document** > **Import Repo**.

    Enter your repo URL like `https://github.com/user/my-docs` and import.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
